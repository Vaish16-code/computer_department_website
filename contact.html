Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get admission to the Computer Engineering program?

Admission information is available through our admissions office. You can contact admissions.cse@college.edu or visit the main admissions office for detailed information about eligibility, application process, and important dates.

What are the visiting hours for faculty members?

Faculty members have designated office hours which are typically posted on their office doors. You can also contact the department office to schedule an appointment or check faculty availability.

How can I get information about placement opportunities?

For placement-related queries, please contact our Placement Coordinator at placements.cse@college.edu or visit the placement office during working hours.

Can I visit the department labs and facilities?
